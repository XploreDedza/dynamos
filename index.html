About the Club

Built on Pride. Forged in Dedza.

Goshen City Dedza Dynamos FC was founded in 2024 with one mission — to bring top-flight football to Dedza and represent the heart of Central Malawi. In our debut TNM Super League season, we've shown the nation that the Dynamos are here to stay.

Yellow and black isn't just our colours — it's our identity. Our commitment to the community, to youth development, and to winning football drives everything we do.
